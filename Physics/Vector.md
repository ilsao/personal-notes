# 單詞

- Equilibrant: 平衡力，即大小相等，方向相反。
- Exert: 施力。

# 例題

1. 
   A sail boat follows a series of racing buoys. On the first lap it goes 8 mi at $20^{\circ}$, then 10 mi at $40^{\circ}$, then 6 mi at $130^{\circ}$. What distance is it from its starting point, and in what direction must it sail to return?

Sol:
$x = 8\cos 20^{\circ} + 10\cos 40^{\circ} + 6\cos 130^{\circ} \approx 11.321$
$y = 8\sin 20^{\circ}+10\cos 40^{\circ} + 6\sin 130^{\circ} \approx 13.760$

distance $\approx \sqrt{ (11.321)^{2}+(13.76)^{2} } \approx 17.8 mi$
direction $\approx \arctan\left( \frac{13.76}{11.321} \right) \approx 50.55^{\circ}$
home direction = $180^{\circ}$ + direction = $230.55^{\circ}$

2. 
   一個箱子被 A, B, C, 推，其中 A 相對 x 軸 $37^{\circ}$ 施力 50lb，B 相對 x 軸 $-30^{\circ}$ 施力 50lb。求 C 所施的力與方向，使得箱子僅受水平方向的力 90lb。

Sol:
首先求得 AB 兩人所施合力。
$x = 5\cos 37^{\circ}+50\cos 30^{\circ} \approx 82.23$
$y = 50\sin 37^{\circ} - 50 \sin 30^{\circ} \approx 5.09$

然後設 C 所施的力為 $F$ ，方向為$\theta$。則：
$F\cos \theta + 83.23 = 90$
$F\sin \theta + 5.09 = 0$
$\implies F \approx 8.47, \theta \approx -37^{\circ}$
