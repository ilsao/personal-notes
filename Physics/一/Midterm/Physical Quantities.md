# 例題

The radius of a carbon atom is about $2.5 \times 10^{-8}$ cm. Neglect the volume lost in packing the spheres; Answer following questions. 
(a) How many could fit in a row 1-cm long?
(b) How many could fit in a layer one atom deep and area $1cm^{2}$?
(c) How many could fit in a cube 1 cm on each side?
(d) If a crystal of carbon atoms(diamond) had this form, what is the minimum number of impurity atoms that could block the light coming **through** the faces of a 1-$cm^{3}$ cube? Express your answer in both percent and in parts per million. (Hint: Assume as an approximation that a layer of impurity atoms on each of three faces of the cube at right angles to each other could block all the light)

Sol:

(a)
2r = $5 \times 10^{-8}$cm
$\frac{1cm}{5\times 10^{-8}cm}=2 \times 10^{7}$ (個)

(b)
因為 "Neglect the volume lost in packing the spheres" 所以將球型的原子視為立方體。否利用球形計算明顯不符合 "Neglect the volume lost" 的成立條件。
$\frac{5 \times 10^{-8}cm \times 1cm^{2}}{(5 \times 10^{-8}cm)^{3}}=4 \times 10^{14}$ (個)

(c)
$\frac{1cm^{3}}{(5\times 10^{-8}cm)^{3}}=8\times 10^{21}$ (個)

(d)
注意關鍵詞 "through"，因為只需要擋住其中互相垂直的三面，光線就必不可能穿越。
擋住三面所需要的原子數：$3 \times 4 \times 10^{14}＝ 1.2\times 10^{15}$ (個)

百分比：$\frac{1.2 \times 10^{15}}{8 \times 10^{21}}=1.5 \times 10^{-5}$ %。

回想 ppm 的定義：百萬分之一，即 $10^{-6}$。
所以 ppm：$1.5 \times 10^{-7} \times 10^{6}$ ppm。
